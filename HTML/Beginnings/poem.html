Ah, a beautiful day in the sky!
The sun is shining and the birds are singing
I know today will be a good day
It is hot but the heat will not ruin today

I walk down the street past the zooming cars
With their loud engines and speeding tires
I cross the street to my bus stop where the clouds are
They look so fluffy like cotton in my pillow

Today is definietly a beautiful day after all!
