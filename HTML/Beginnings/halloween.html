What is Halloween all about?

Transformation

One of Halloween's main themes is transformation. During autumn, many things change from the weather to people. For example after harvest, the eariler crops wither and die once the weather turns cold. An example of this is the pumpkin despite being the icon of Halloween. When people carve pumpkins into jack-o-laterns, there is a change because the pumpkin is alive until it is cut thus becoming dead. Another crop that transforms is corn. During the hot months, we know corn grows in a cron field. When the fall comes corn is harvested from their big stalks.

Mystery

Another theme of Halloween is mystery especially during the season. It is said during Halloween, unexplained things happen. Things go bump into the night while others can hear a witch's cackle in the forest. People get into many accidents they can not explain such toes stubs, arm pinches when no one is there, and falling objects at random places. Another thing when it comes to mystery is unexplained deaths. Halloween is often one of most popular time for deaths. This theme goes hand in hand with supernatual elements.
